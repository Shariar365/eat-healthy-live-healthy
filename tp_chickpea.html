chickpea salad recipe

Ingredients:

  • Vegetables: Such as broccoli, cucumber, lettuce, tomato
  • Chickpeas.(Chickpeas are also known as garbanzo beans)
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Fresh parsley
  • Avacado
  • Lemon juice


Instruction:

Cut a ripe avocado and toss with fresh lemon juice per the recipe below. Add remaining ingredients, including rinsed chickpeas. Whisk dressing ingredients in a small bowl and drizzle over the salad. Gently toss. Season with salt & pepper to taste. This salad will last days in the fridge making for a great lunch dish!
